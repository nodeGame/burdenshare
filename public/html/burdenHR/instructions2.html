There will be two stages of the game. Stage One consists of five rounds. In each round, you will decide whether you want high, medium, or low growth in your economy. All levels of growth will increase your wealth. However, the exact amount is uncertain and can be any number between the following ranges.

If you choose: Your wealth increase (in this round):
Low growth Between 1 and 10 ECU
Medium growth Between 5 and 15 ECU
High Growth Between 10 and 20 ECU


The economic growth in your economy will contribute to climate risk. Climate risk is the probability that you loose part of your wealth as a result of a climate catastrophe in the second stage of the experiment. Your climate risk will increase depending on your economic growth as follows:

For each round that you chose: Climate risk increases by:
Low growth 0%
Medium growth 2.5%
High Growth 5%
