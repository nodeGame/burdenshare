INSTRUCTIONS: Please read carefully



You will play four rounds. Each of these rounds is completely independent from the others, and you will start each round with the same wealth and contribution to climate risk.

You will start with a PRACTICE round. This will not be included in the experiment. Each part of the round will be explained. The subsequent three rounds will be used in the experiment. If you have questions in these rounds, you can access these instructions at any time by clicking on the button at the bottom of the screen labeled "Click here to show or hide the instructions".

At the end of the game, you will be paid your remaining endowment from one of the three rounds of the experiment in addition to the fixed show-up fee. The payout round is randomly selected by the computer at the end of the game. The conversion rate is 50 ECU to 1 USD.

After the game, there will be a short questionnaire. Your payment does not depend on your answers in the questionnaire.

Attention

To ensure that you receive your pay:
  • Always follow the instructions on the screen.
  • Do not use the "Back Button" of your browser during the experiment.
  • If you mistakenly use the "Back Button", please follow the instructions on the opening page and click "Continue".
  • If your browser crashes, you have 60 seconds to reconnect, otherwise the experiment will be terminated. To reconnect, reopen your browser and copy the internet address that you can see now in your address bar.
We will be unable to pay you unless you follow these guidelines.
